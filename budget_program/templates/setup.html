Budget Setup

Let's build your plan step by step.

1. Your accounts

How much money do you have right now? Add each account — checking, savings, HYSA, Roth IRA, brokerage, cash.

2. Your debts

Add what you owe — student loans, car loan, credit cards, medical. No debt? Just continue.

3. Your goals

What are you working toward? e.g. reach $10k in your HYSA, max your Roth IRA, or pay off a debt by a date.

4. Recurring spending

Consistent bills and subscriptions — Netflix, Amazon Prime, gym, phone, rent. These are reserved off the top before budgeting.

5. Your income

If you earn a steady amount, set it here so we can pace your goals and project your budget. Irregular income (tips, gig work)? Skip this — you can log money whenever it lands.

Leave the amount blank to skip and rely on logging income as you receive it.

6. Budget split

After subscriptions and goal savings are reserved, the rest is split across these categories. Percentages must total 100%.
